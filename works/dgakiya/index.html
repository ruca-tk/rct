DG Camp Akiya

Web Design, Visual Direction & Front-End Development

DG Camp Akiya introduces a coastal co-working and community space. Excluding the logo, I led the web design, visual direction, drone footage planning, and full front-end implementation, translating the atmosphere of the physical location into a cohesive digital experience.

View Site

Scope

  • UI/UX design
  • Visual system design
  • Drone footage direction and shot planning
  • Image planning and coordination
  • Front-end architecture (HTML / CSS / JavaScript)
  • Animation design and implementation
  • Responsive development

Challenges

  • Conveying spatial atmosphere through digital presentation
  • Aligning aerial footage with the site's visual structure
  • Balancing immersive visuals with informational clarity
  • Maintaining performance despite media-heavy content

Approach

  • Structured the site to guide users through concept, location, and space
  • Directed drone shots to capture scale, landscape, and architectural context
  • Designed layouts to integrate video seamlessly within the narrative
  • Optimized media delivery to preserve performance across devices

Results

The site delivers a cohesive digital representation of the physical environment. Drone footage reinforces the sense of scale and location. Visual structure and motion support an immersive yet clear experience. The implementation remains stable and performant despite media-heavy assets.
